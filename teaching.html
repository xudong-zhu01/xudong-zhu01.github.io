Current Experience

HI2520: Global Empire in the Long Nineteenth Century

University of Aberdeen | Academic Year 2025-2026 Teaching Assistant

The long nineteenth century saw dramatic rises and falls in power systems bringing together a range of peoples and territories. This course examines this phenomenon which, for better or worse, made the modern world.

Responsibilities:

  • Leading weekly tutorial sessions for undergraduate students.
  • Facilitating discussions on primary and secondary source analysis.
  • Supporting students in developing critical thinking and writing skills.

HI1022: Europe in the Twentieth Century

University of Aberdeen | Academic Year 2025-2026 Teaching Assistant

A comprehensive examination of European history throughout the twentieth century, exploring major political, social, and cultural transformations.

Responsibilities:

  • Leading weekly tutorial sessions and facilitating source analysis.
  • Providing feedback on essays and research assignments.
  • Holding office hours for individual student consultations.
